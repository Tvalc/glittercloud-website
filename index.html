The journey

From Nintendo call-center analytics to founding Glittercloud — and every launch in between.

2009 — The foundation

Nintendo of America

Customer analyst · operations & technology

I joined the video games industry as a customer analyst, onboarding new technology to make support operations smarter — including a speech analytics program to analyze and optimize call-center performance and IVR routing.

  • Helped lead the integration of Oracle with Slalom consulting
  • Built the operational mindset I still use: measure, route, improve, repeat

2011 – 2015

Amazon · Video games marketing

Leadership roles in retail video games marketing

I spent the bulk of my career at Amazon. On the retail video games team I pioneered community management and direct-to-consumer sales motions — including one of the first charity affiliate relationships on Reddit’s /r/gamedeals, a program that still exists today and, when I left, was driving hundreds of thousands of dollars in charitable donations through game sales on Amazon.com.

  • Established community-led growth before it was a playbook every startup copies
  • Moved to Appstore & Game Studios — helped launch mobile titles including Saber’s Edge and Til Morning’s Light

2015

EA Sports

Senior product manager · loyalty & esports

I took a brief break from Amazon to launch the loyalty program for Madden Ultimate Team — MUT Rewards — a program thousands of players joined, engaging more deeply and spending more heavily on Ultimate Team.

  • Concept through GTM: branding, business modeling, KPI development, and product delivery
  • Proof that retention systems — not just acquisition — drive revenue in live-service games

2016 – 2019

Amazon · Twitch Prime founding team

Marketing lead · 0 → 10M+ members

A huge opportunity pulled me back to Amazon: lead marketing for a brand-new product, Twitch Prime. I joined the founding team and grew it from zero users and myself as the only marketer to more than 10 million members and a 12-person marketing team.

  • First Prime benefit launched simultaneously in seven countries
  • Expanded to 200+ countries; 40+ co-marketing campaigns with Fortnite, Hearthstone, Call of Duty, Madden, and more
  • Led 15 marketers and UX designers at peak scale

2019 – 2021

Seattle startups · Leafly & Convoy

Marketing leader · B2B & digital growth

I left Amazon to help Seattle startups move faster. At Leafly I launched the first international Budtender Tour — a sponsored event that drove ad sales across the platform and B2B revenue for advertising partners. At Convoy I led digital marketing through a full website rebuild: first as an independent contributor, then scaling delivery through a professional agency as team leader.

  • Leafly: grew marketing from 5 to 20 people in three months; first $1M 4/20 campaign
  • Convoy: team of 8; 100%+ increase in qualified leads after relaunch

2021 – Today

Founder · GTM lead · AAA launches

Glittercloud Solutions & select engagements

I founded my own company and led marketing for high-profile game launches — including Shrapnel (the $SHRAP token reached a $1B market cap with $20M+ daily trading volume at peak), Wildcard, Sparkball, and The Wanderers. In parallel I served as GTM lead for Crystal Dynamics on Legacy of Kain Remastered, working directly with the Chief Strategy Officer.

  • Glittercloud: $0 → $1M+ consulting ARR; launches across remasters, indie, and live-service titles
  • Shrapnel: 23K → 360K on X; $500K+ pre-sales 12+ months before release; partnerships with Razer, Puma, Alpine F1
  • Corporate scale and startup scrappiness — in the same career, often in the same quarter
